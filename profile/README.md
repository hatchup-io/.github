# Hatchup

**Hatchup** is a capital company that backs early-stage startups with funding, infrastructure, and hands-on operational services. We don't just write checks — we build, deploy, and scale alongside our founders.

Our work is organized across four divisions.

---

## Platform

The shared technical and financial backbone that every Hatchup division runs on — capital management, identity, payments, and the common services that let our products ship faster.

- [`hatchup`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/hatchup) · [`hatchup-backend`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/hatchup-backend) · [`hatchup-frontend`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/hatchup-frontend) — the core Hatchup platform
- [`iam-platform`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/iam-platform) — identity and access management across all products
- [`hatchup-payment-system`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/hatchup-payment-system) — payments and billing
- [`production`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/production) — Ansible playbooks that deploy our stacks to Docker Swarm
- [`django-boilerplate`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/django-boilerplate) — the starter template every backend service is bootstrapped from

## Foundry — Venture Studio

Foundry is where Hatchup incubates its own startups. Each product below is a company we founded, funded, and built from zero.

- **[Katavex](https://github.com/hatchup-io/katavex-backend)** — AI-assisted OKR and performance management platform that keeps organizational goals alive across teams instead of turning them into static documents.
- **[Phronexia](https://github.com/hatchup-io/phronexia)** — enterprise decision-making engine that combines LLMs with Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM) to turn unstructured inputs into quantifiable, explainable rankings. ([frontend](https://github.com/hatchup-io/phronexia-frontend))
- **[Pathwise](https://github.com/hatchup-io/pathwise_backend)** — AI-powered immigration consultant that guides applicants through complex legal and procedural pathways. ([design bundle](https://github.com/hatchup-io/pathwise))
- **[Ordnix](https://github.com/hatchup-io/ordnix-backend)** — workflow and order management product. ([frontend](https://github.com/hatchup-io/ordnix-frontend))
- **[Adapt-lab](https://github.com/hatchup-io/ragbot)** — multi-tenant RAG chatbot platform that lets businesses train a personalized assistant on their own documents and embed it into their landing pages.

## LaunchPad

LaunchPad is Hatchup's onboarding arm for founders entering the UAE market — business registration, free-zone setup, compliance, and the first operational steps of running a company in the region.

- [`LaunchPad`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/LaunchPad) — product UI
- [`launchpad-backend-project`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/launchpad-backend-project) — registration, onboarding, and compliance backend
- [`launchpad-landing-front`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/launchpad-landing-front) — public marketing site

## AdaptLab

AdaptLab builds bespoke software products for external clients — custom platforms that external companies hire us to design, build, and operate.

- **yFace** — face-proportion analysis and aesthetic-surgery recommendation platform powered by Face Mesh and LLMs, with dedicated panels for patients and medical bloggers. Repos: [`yface-ai`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/yface-ai) · [`yface-backend`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/yface-backend) · [`yface-frontend`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/yface-frontend) · [`yface-patient-panel`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/yface-patient-panel) · [`yface-patient-panel-backend`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/yface-patient-panel-backend) · [`yface-blogger-panel`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/yface-blogger-panel)
- **MatchPointIQ** — B2B matchmaking and deal-management platform that connects companies, sources suppliers via scraping and vector search, and handles contracts and compliance end-to-end. Repos: [`b2b-backend-project`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/b2b-backend-project) · [`b2b-frontend`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/b2b-frontend) · [`b2b-matchmaking`](https://github.com/hatchup-io/b2b-matchmaking)
